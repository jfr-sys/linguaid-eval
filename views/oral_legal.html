Étape 1 / 5 — Compréhension orale

Évaluation de la compréhension orale

Évaluez la capacité du candidat à comprendre sans reformulation ni simplification dans un contexte juridique anglophone.

Suggestions de prompts — contexte juridique

A2–B1Describe a simple client situation in English and ask them to summarise what the problem is.
B1–B2Read a short clause or contractual provision; ask them to explain what it means and what the implications are.
B2Describe a negotiation scenario (e.g. disputed termination clause) and ask what questions they would ask the other party.
B2–C1Read a short legal opinion extract; ask them to agree or push back and explain their reasoning.

Suggestions de prompts — anglais juridique

C1"Introduce yourself professionally as if meeting a foreign client for the first time."
C2"A client calls: they've just been served with a breach of contract claim. Conduct the initial intake interview."
C3"You've reviewed the case. Advise the client on their options and the pros and cons of each."
C6"Role-play: negotiate a key clause — e.g. limitation of liability — with the opposing party."
